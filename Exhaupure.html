How ExhauPure Transforms Emissions

1. Intake & Initial Filtration

Raw exhaust gases enter the ExhauPure system. Larger soot particles are immediately captured by the pre-filter mesh.

2. Nanofiber Core Activation

Gases then pass through the proprietary nanofiber core, where electrostatic attraction and molecular sieving trap ultra-fine particulates and toxic compounds like NOx and CO.

3. Catalytic Conversion

A specialized catalytic converter within the filter converts remaining harmful gases into inert substances like nitrogen, water vapor, and carbon dioxide.

4. Oxygen Enrichment Chamber

A final chamber introduces a controlled amount of fresh air, aiding in the complete oxidation of trace pollutants and slightly enriching the output with oxygen.

5. Clean Air Release

Significantly purified air, with vastly reduced pollutants, is released back into the atmosphere, contributing to cleaner urban environments.
